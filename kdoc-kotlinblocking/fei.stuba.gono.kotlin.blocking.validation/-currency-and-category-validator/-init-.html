CurrencyAndCategoryValidator()

Class implementing validation of CurrencyAndCategory annotation for ReportedOverlimitTransaction. Transaction cannot have Category DOMESTIC and use non EUR currency or have category FX and currency EUR. Trieda implementujúca validáciu objektu triedy ReportedOverlimitTransaction s anotáciou CurrencyAndCategory. Transakcia nesmie mať kategóriu (orderCategory premennú) OrderCategory.DOMESTIC a menu (amount.currency) inú ako Currency.EUR alebo kategóriu FX a nie menu EUR.
